Can you really use me?
Jesus spoke of a church as a body, not a building. And that body was made up of many different people with many different talent and abilities. And while the world we live in has chosen to not value certain passions and abilities, the church (or should we say connexion) is the place where we believe every single person and their unique set of strengths, talents and abilities is crucial to a healthy community.
