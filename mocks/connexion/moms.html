Mom's ARISE !

What is Mom's Arise ?
Moms Arise! is a new program for moms who feel disadvantaged,alone and overwhelmed by life. At Moms Arise! you will:

  • Connect
    ...meet other moms from the community who face similar personal, financial and parenting challenges. The program will help you to build a network of women who can provide friendship during the good times and the hard times.
  • Grow
    ...take part in weekly workshops that will help you to manage yourself, your family, and your finances more effectively. Each workshop is focused on providing you with relevant information and skills which you can use in your everyday life.
  • Thrive
    ...meet with a caring mentor who will listen, provide guidance and encourage you as you achieve your goals. As you work one-to-one with your mentor you will be able to share your daily struggles and successes and talk about how to put your new skills into practice. Your mentor will also be able to provide spiritual support and prayer.

Is Moms Arise! right for you ?
If you ever feel this way then Moms Arise! is right for you:

  • I sometimes feel disadvantaged, isolated and alone as a mother
  • I wish I had someone to talk to about my personal, financial and parenting struggles
  • I would like to learn new skills to help me manage my life and my family better

A Children’s Program is offered so you can relax and enjoy your time in the workshops. We have a team of dedicated child-care workers who will do activities and play with your children in a safe, child-friendly environment. Snacks are provided too.
