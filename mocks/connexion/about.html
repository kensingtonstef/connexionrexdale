about
connexion

Connexion is a community of imperfect people growing in connexion to eachother, our community and God.It is a new church in the Rexdale Neighbourhood of Toronto.

For us, it's all about, all because of, all for... Jesus. Its not about buildings with amazing architecture, how nice you can dress, or your personal list of accomplishments. We want to be the church Jesus had in mind and he never saw a church a place to go, but as a people...not a religious intitution but a movement of faith, love and hope.

We think this whole faith thing is at its best when it motivates selfservice, sacrifice and love... caring about the things God cares about.

We were all created to live for something larger than ourselves and God invites everyone, everywhere into this way of living.

We would love to connect with you at our next experience, Sunday August 19th at 11am, in the auditorium at St.Stephens school.
